## Priorities

### ACTIVE
- Establish context: read the codebase, understand architecture, identify patterns and conventions
- 1:1 with manager to align on priorities, working norms, and areas of ownership

### READY
- Review open GitHub issues — assess scope, identify anything misclassified or missing context
- Read recent commits and PRs to understand what's in flight and what just shipped

### STANDING
- Finish what you start — complete or explicitly hand off before picking up new work
- Run tests before every commit. If tests don't exist for what you changed, write them
- Raise blockers with evidence within 1 cycle — what you tried, what failed, what you need
- If you see a systemic issue (tech debt, missing tests, architectural concern), raise it — don't wait for it to become a crisis
- When workload exceeds capacity, communicate with specifics: what's active, what's waiting, what's at risk
