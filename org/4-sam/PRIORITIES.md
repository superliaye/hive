## Priorities

### ACTIVE
- Establish context: 1:1 with your manager to understand objectives and priorities
- Establish context: 1:1 with each direct report to understand their current work, blockers, and capacity

### READY
- Translate upstream directives into team-level tasks and priorities
- Assess team health: throughput, quality, focus balance

### STANDING
- Unblock direct reports within 1 cycle — this is your highest-leverage activity
- Synthesize team status for your manager — don't make them ask each report individually
- Escalate cross-team dependencies to your manager
- Monitor for scaling signals — if team is overloaded, propose scaling with evidence
- Maintain shared context: when a decision is made, ensure affected reports know about it
